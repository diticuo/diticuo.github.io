Buzzer Quiz


Description:

This event aims to measure your analytical, technical, your ability to work as a team and the knowledge of participant’s beyond academics. The participants who are interested in excellency are heartly welcome.


General Rules & Regulations:

  • A team must have two members.
  • For answering the question first press the Buzzer and then answer.
  • Based on performance teams will progress to the next round.
  • Use of mobile phones or any electronic gadgets are not allowed.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • Any murmuring while asking question’s to other team will not be allowed.
  • No argument with any other team or host will be allowed.
  • Any Indiscipline would eventually result to the disqualification of team.

Round 1 Q for Curious:

  • Based on current trend in Computer Science we will ask few questions to every team and they have to answer in limited time.

Round 2 Guess What? :

  • We will present some logo or picture of popular organization’s who have some significant contribution in field of Computer Science and team’s have to answer them.

Round 3 Rapid Fire :

  • Member from each team will come and pick one chit. Question’s will be asked based on that chit topic and team can answer by pressing the Buzzer. If not able to answer, question will be passed to next team.

NOTE:

  • In Round 2 if team is not able to answer then they can demand for some clue from the host. From second hint the marks will be deducted.
  • In Round 3 if two team press the Buzzer at the same time both of them will be given chance to answer the question. If both team answer are correct marks will be divided evenly.
  • For every round team member can discuss within them to come up with one final Answer.
  • Based on time and circumstances the round may differ.
