Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is OPTIMA ?

Optima is a national level technical symposium conducted by Department of Computer Applications, National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli. This year it is being conducted in month of October from 19 October to 20 October.

Who can join us?

Any undergraduate or postgraduate student who has interest in Computer Science. It is not necessary that only computer science background student can join us, anyone can be a part of it.

What is the eligibility criteria to be a participant of the programme?

Campus Ambassador programme is an exclusive opportunity for the college/university students to gain valuable experience of being an Ambassador of his/her college/university who are willing to do something different.

What is the Campus Ambassador programme?

Campus Ambassador programme is an exclusive opportunity for the college/university students to gain valuable experience of being an Ambassador of his/her college/university who are willing to do something different.

What are the benefits of being Campus Ambassador?

Campus Ambassador, supposed to be a coordinator for students who are willing to be participants of the programme. Besides this there are many opportunities of being Campus Ambassador like internship opportunities, incentives, etc. Being a College Ambassador will enhance your communication and managerial skills, increase contacts, enhance your image and represent your college with great experience.

Who can attend workshop?

Anybody who is a part of Optima can attend the workshop.

How to reach NIT Tiruchirappalli?

TRAINS- You can directly find some trains to Tiruchirappalli railway station from any major railway station then go to Trichy central bus stand and take a bus from Trichy to Thanjavur, most of the buses that go to Thanjavur will stop at NIT-T main gate or you can book a cab too from railway station.

FLIGHT- You can catch direct flight for Tiruchirappalli airport and take a bus or cab to reach NIT Trichy.

Where to stay(Accommodation)?

There will be arrangement for accommodation of participants coming for this event by the organisers. So no need to be worry about it.

Do I need to arrange food?

No. Food coupons will be provided.

OPTIMA

Optima, India’s largest annual techno-management symposium of NIT Tiruchirappalli, celebrates 12 glorious years of its making. Since its inception in 2007, Optima has been providing a national platform to youth for showcasing their talent. It has organized a plethora of events for its participants, spanning through genres encompassing the fields of technology and management, to compete in.This time stepping into the twelfth year, Optima is going to be a two days symposium comprising of workshop and hacakthon.

THEME

Computer science plays an important role in space technology, The natural tendency and curiosity of the human brain to explore, to discover and reveal the mysteries of this universe. India space program started with a modest beginning in the 1960s , It has grown steadily, achieving significant milestones. These include fabrication of satellites, space-launch vehicles, and a range of associated capabilities With growing confidence, ISRO has also started to undertake more ambitious space science and exploration missions. The most notable of these have been the Chandrayaan and the Mangalyaan missions. Recently India has taken its space expedition to a whole new level with chandrayaan 2. Through this years theme , Optima aims to create awareness about India's potential and stake in global space economy with a emphasis on importance of computer science in space technology, Join us in exploring these horizons and celebrating it Welcome to Optima'19.

Hackathon


Description:

Hackathon is a great opportunity to expose your talent and knowledge on your research areas. Bring your innovative ideas and we are ready to appreciate it. We provide you a platform to present your thoughts that would enlighten people and also that would create a significant development in technology. All you need to do is create a presentation and showcase a working model to present your idea.


Topics:

You can choose any topic, where you think technology can aid humanity or nature.

General Rules & Regulations:

  • The registered participants are not allowed to change their preference after sending the paper (Abstract) to us.
  • The paper is not supposed to be the exact replica of the one that is found to be available either in web or in any other source; otherwise it will be rejected.
  • Maximum number of slides is 20
  • Every team will have minimum of three and maximum of five member
  • The presentation should be in .ppt format
  • You will be provided with a laptop with Microsoft Office preinstalled for presentation.
  • For demonstration bring your own laptops and other necessary devices
  • After the phase of scrutiny and selection, only deserving papers will be shortlisted for the final round.

Evaluation:

  • Novelty of your project.
  • Impact of your project on the world
  • Progress so far.

Deadlines:

  • Submission Date (prelims) -5th October, 2019
  • Shortlisted Announcement -12th October, 2019
  • Event date -19/20th October, 2019

Prelims:

  • Abstract must be sent earlier in PDF format, create a small video of your working model and send it.
  • Videos should be in .mp4 format and its duration should be less than 10 mins.
  • Mail the abstract and video in optima@nitt.edu

Presentation:

  • Every team will get maximum of twenty minutes to present their ideas.
  • 10 mins(Presenting idea)+5 mins(Demo)+5mins(Q&A session)
  • Final decision will be taken by the judges

Schedule:

  • Date: 19/20th October, 2019
