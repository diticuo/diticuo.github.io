Hackathon


Description:

Hackathon is a great opportunity to expose your talent and knowledge on your research areas. Bring your innovative ideas and we are ready to appreciate it. We provide you a platform to present your thoughts that would enlighten people and also that would create a significant development in technology. All you need to do is create a presentation and showcase a working model to present your idea.


Topics:

You can choose any topic, where you think technology can aid humanity or nature.

General Rules & Regulations:

  • The registered participants are not allowed to change their preference after sending the paper (Abstract) to us.
  • The paper is not supposed to be the exact replica of the one that is found to be available either in web or in any other source; otherwise it will be rejected.
  • Maximum number of slides is 20
  • Every team will have minimum of three and maximum of five member
  • The presentation should be in .ppt format
  • You will be provided with a laptop with Microsoft Office preinstalled for presentation.
  • For demonstration bring your own laptops and other necessary devices
  • After the phase of scrutiny and selection, only deserving papers will be shortlisted for the final round.

Evaluation:

  • Novelty of your project.
  • Impact of your project on the world
  • Progress so far.

Deadlines:

  • Submission Date (prelims) -5th October, 2019
  • Shortlisted Announcement -12th October, 2019
  • Event date -19/20th October, 2019

Prelims:

  • Abstract must be sent earlier in PDF format, create a small video of your working model and send it.
  • Videos should be in .mp4 format and its duration should be less than 10 mins.
  • Mail the abstract and video in optima@nitt.edu

Presentation:

  • Every team will get maximum of twenty minutes to present their ideas.
  • 10 mins(Presenting idea)+5 mins(Demo)+5mins(Q&A session)
  • Final decision will be taken by the judges
