Treasure Hunt


Description:

Treasure hunt is a fun activity that compels you to unleash your potential to solve mind numbing riddles under the pressure of a ticking clock. If the thought of that is less exciting and more frightening to you then don't worry there'll be fun exercises and it'll allow you to feel the joy of the great outdoors. It also gives you a platform to explore the vast NIT-T campus in the most exhilarating way possible. Try your best to find hidden objects or places by following clever clues that don't even require you to have any previous knowledge about the campus. And in the unlikeliest of scenarios, if you manage to get yourself lost, then you'll get to experience the true gem of NIT-T, it's kind and welcoming people.


General Rules & Regulations:

  • Participants must have sense of adventure and an open mind.
  • Each team may have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 participants.
  • Teams will be provided with a map of campus. Teams cannot access restricted zones marked on the map.
  • Teams must be at the same clue site at a time and you must stay within sight or earshot of each other.
  • Use of electronic devices and other gadgets is strictly prohibited.
  • Do not move, tamper with, destroy, or otherwise alter the answers at the clue locations.
  • If anything makes you think, "Is this cheating?", it probably is. Don't do it. If anything makes you think, "Is this a really clever loophole?", it's probably cheating. Don't do it.
  • First three teams which qualifies the first round will be eligible to enter the final round.
  • Final decision will be taken by the judges.
  • Further rules will be communicated at the time of the event.
