Technical Debate


Description:

This event is to challenge the participants on their intuitive creativity and ability in technical streams. It is the visual edge to showcase your skills in technical impugnment.


General Rules :

  • Total 6 teams each consists of 2 members.
  • No participant can be a part of more than 1 team.
  • 3 teams for affirmative side and 3 teams for opposing side.
  • Affirmative and opposing teams will come in alternate order.
  • Debate will be only in English.

Guidelines:

  • Anyone speaker of one of the affirmative teams will present arguments in support of the resolution (max 10 min).
  • Anyone speaker of one of the opposing teams will present arguments opposing the resolution (max 10 min).
  • Now 3 minutes will be given to the teams to prepare their mutilation.
  • Each opposing team will begin with the mutilation, attempting to defend the opposing arguments and to defeat the supporting arguments without adding any new information (max 2 min).
  • Each affirmative team will go with the mutilation, attempting to defend the supporting arguments and to defeat the opposing arguments without adding any new information (max 2 min).
  • Now, in audience if anybody will want to ask questions to any of the teams, then any of the two members of the team may reply.

NOTE:

  • Topics will be notified later.
  • There must not be any interruptions. Speakers must wait until their turns come.
  • Each group must follow the time limit strictly.
